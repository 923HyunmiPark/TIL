4. GROUP BY : 데이터로 요약
    * GROUP BY에 들어가는 것은 무조건 SELECT에도 들어가야한다!!

 ```SQL
 SELECT Company
     , AVG(Salary) as AVERAGE
     , SUM(Salary) as SUMMATION
FROM www_salary
GROUP BY Company
```
